Petrol Station Management

Technologies Used: HTML CSS JavaScript

About This Project

Petrol Station Management is a robust system designed to streamline operations at fuel stations. It helps manage fuel inventory, monitor sales, generate reports, and oversee staff activities, all from a centralized dashboard.

  • Real-time fuel inventory tracking and alerts
  • Automated sales and billing management
  • Employee scheduling and shift management
  • Comprehensive analytics and reporting tools
  • Secure, multi-role access for managers and staff
Empowering petrol stations with efficient, data-driven management and seamless daily operations.
